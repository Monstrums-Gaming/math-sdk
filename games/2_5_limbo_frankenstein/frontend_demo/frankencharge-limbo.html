HOW TO PLAY — LIMBO

  • Pick a bet tier (BASE ×1, STREAK ×2, HIGH ×100 stake), a bet amount, and a target multiplier.
  • Press CHARGE. The machine generates a result multiplier that starts at 1.00x.
  • If the result is equal to or higher than your target, you win stake × target (stake = bet × tier).
  • Higher targets pay more but hit less often. Win chances are the published RGS math (~96.6% RTP); the tier only scales your stake.

This is a demo with play money that replays the real math-sdk build (limbo_rgs.json). Reset your balance any time.

RULES

  • Each target is a published base mode. You win if result ≥ target; a win pays stake × target (stake = bet × tier: ×1 / ×2 / ×100).
  • Win/lose is drawn against the mode's published win probability from the RGS lookup table (so realised RTP ≈ 96.6%), then a provably-fair crash number is shown consistent with the outcome.
  • Only the BASE tier is published; STREAK/HIGH just scale your stake over the same odds (bet-size scaling is an ACP bet-level concern, not a separate mode). The slider snaps to the nearest published target.
  • Every mode's RTP sits in Stake's 96.00%–96.70% band; payouts are on the 0.1x grid.
  • Stake (bet × tier) is deducted when you charge. Minimum bet 1.00.

PROVABLY FAIR

Each roll is derived from SHA-256(serverSeed : clientSeed : nonce). The hash of the server seed is shown before you roll, so results can't be manipulated after the fact.
